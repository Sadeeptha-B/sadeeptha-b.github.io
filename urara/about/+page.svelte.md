You can check out my work over at [GitHub](https://github.com/Sadeeptha-B)!

When I'm not working you can find me watching movies, reading, vibing to music or sightseeing.

I'm interested in sharing my knowledge with the community and honing my skillset. You can find my thoughts on a series of topics that I find interesting in this personal blog.
